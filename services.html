Campus Services and Resources

Student Support and Accessibility

We are dedicated to ensuring our PA students have the resources necessary to succeed both academically and personally.

Academic Support

  • Medical Library: 24/7 access to physical and digital medical databases, journals, and textbooks.
  • Tutoring Services: Peer-led and faculty-guided tutoring sessions available upon request.
  • Disability Accommodations: The Office of Student Accessibility provides tailored accommodations for documented disabilities.

Health and Wellness

  • Student Health Clinic: Acute and preventative care services located on the main campus.
  • Mental Health Counseling: Free, confidential counseling services ensuring timely access for personal issues.

Distant Campus Location Policy

In accordance with ARC-PA standards, the University PA Program operates exclusively on the main campus. Therefore, all student services, faculty office hours, and academic resources are equally available to all enrolled students. The program does not utilize a geographically distant campus location.
