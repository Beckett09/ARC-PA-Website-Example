Clinical Site Placement Policy

Site Solicitation Prohibition: The University PA Program handles all aspects of clinical site and preceptor development. PA students are never required to provide or solicit clinical sites or preceptors.

Student Travel Policy: Students may be required to travel significant distances or relocate for mandatory core clinical rotations. Coordination and funding for transportation and lodging are the sole responsibility of the student.
