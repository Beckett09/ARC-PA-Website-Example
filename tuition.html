Direct Costs (Program Tuition)

Clinical Travel & Relocation Policy

During the clinical phase, students are responsible for all living, travel, & relocation expenses associated with clinical rotations. These costs are highly variable & are estimated to be between $5,000 - $8,000 depending on rotation site placement.
